Data.Quaternion
Description

Quaternion (a.k.a. 4-dimensional complex numbers) operations, with particular emphasis on the representation of rotations in 3-dimensional space.

Typical quaternion usage in computer graphics is to construct or compose one, such as a 45-degree rotation around the X-axis: let q = angleAxis 45 (1, 0, 0) and then either directly multiply it by a vector like so: toVec (reciprocal q mul fromVec v mul q) or perhaps convert it to a matrix for use with OpenGL: makeMatrix (4, 4) (rowMajorElems q)
